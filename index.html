AI Ping Pong







Instructions

1. First Keep your laptop screen straight

2. Keep yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop

3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist

4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet from the laptop

5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
